Exercise 2

Explain

Two and a half years ago I left my consulting job in San Francisco, moved to Lake Tahoe and spent 6 months figuring out this exact thing. By all accounts I was as happy and well-adjusted as any young professional, but I wanted to undersatnd why I didn't wake up with the same euphoria that I experienced every day in college. After significant deliberation, I discovered that it was because I lacked a sense of community - the feeling that I was part of something bigger than myself. When I was in school, this community was a product of close relationships (a family) that were developed through shared ambition and accomplishments, the desire to help one another, and constant contact (working, living and socializing with the same group, day after day). After Tahoe, I started a company with the sole mission of helping other people and creating that sense of community. Though it's still in its infancy, I hope that one day it grows large enough to re-unite everyone i've ever cared about.
